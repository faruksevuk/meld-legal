This Privacy Policy describes how MELD: AI World Kitchen Recipes ("MELD", "we", "our", or "us"), developed and operated by Faruk Sevük, collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address: Collected when you register or sign in with email. Processed securely via Supabase Auth. We do not store raw passwords.
  • Dietary preferences: Information you provide to personalize recipe generation (e.g., vegan, halal, gluten-free).
  • Serving size preferences: Stored locally and synced to your account.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifier: A unique device ID to manage free trial usage and prevent abuse.
  • App usage data: Recipes generated, saved recipes, in-app interactions.
  • Subscription and purchase history: Managed by RevenueCat to determine your subscription tier.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID): Collected by Google AdMob for free-tier users for personalized ads. You may opt out via device settings.
  • Crash logs and performance data: Collected automatically to identify and fix technical issues.

1.3 AI-Generated Content

MELD uses Google's Gemini API to generate fusion recipes and food imagery. Prompts sent to the AI include your preference selections but do not include your name, email, or personally identifiable information. For disclaimers regarding AI content accuracy and food safety, see our Terms of Service.

4. Advertising and Tracking

Free-tier users may be shown advertisements by Google AdMob, which may use your advertising identifier for personalized ads.

Opt Out of Personalized Ads:

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

Premium subscribers are not shown third-party advertisements.

5. Business Transfers

In the event that MELD or its assets are acquired, merged with, or transferred to another company — including through a sale, acquisition, merger, reorganization, or bankruptcy — your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent in-app notice before your personal information becomes subject to a materially different privacy policy. You will have the opportunity to opt out where required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Permanently removed within 3 days of deletion.
  • Recipes and images: Deleted when removed by you or upon account deletion.
  • Device trial records: Retained up to 12 months for abuse prevention.
  • Crash logs: Retained up to 90 days.
  • Aggregated usage data: May be retained indefinitely in non-identifiable form.

7. Data Security

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Row-level security (RLS) ensuring users access only their own data
  • No storage of raw passwords — authentication handled by Supabase Auth
  • Secure credential management via environment-level secrets

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
