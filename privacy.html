This Privacy Policy describes how MELD: AI World Kitchen Recipes ("MELD", "we", "our", or "us"), developed and operated by Faruk Sevük, collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address: Collected when you register for an account or sign in with email and password.
  • Authentication credentials: Processed securely via Supabase Auth. We do not store raw passwords.
  • Dietary preferences and lifestyle settings: Information you provide to personalize recipe generation (e.g., vegan, halal, gluten-free).
  • Serving size preferences: Stored locally and synced to your account.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device identifier: A unique device ID generated to manage free trial usage and prevent abuse.
  • App usage data: Recipes generated, saved recipes, and general in-app interactions.
  • Subscription and purchase history: Managed by RevenueCat to determine your subscription tier and grant access to premium features.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID): Collected by Google AdMob for free-tier users to display personalized advertisements. You may opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings.
  • Crash logs and performance data: Collected automatically to identify and fix technical issues.
  • IP address and network data: May be collected transiently by our service providers for security and fraud prevention.

1.3 AI-Generated Content

MELD uses Google's Gemini API to generate fusion recipes and food imagery based on your inputs (selected cuisines, meal type, dietary preferences). The prompts sent to the AI include your preference selections but do not include your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. AI-generated content is processed on Google's servers and is subject to Google's privacy practices.

4. Advertising and Tracking

Free-tier users of MELD may be shown advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests.

Your Advertising Choices:

iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track". You can also go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising to limit ad tracking.

Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID or opt out of ads personalization.

Premium subscribers (weekly, monthly, or annual plans) are not shown third-party advertisements.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is scheduled for permanent removal within 3 days.
  • Recipes and images: Deleted when you remove them or when your account is deleted.
  • Device trial records: Retained for abuse prevention purposes for up to 12 months.
  • Crash logs: Retained for up to 90 days.
  • Anonymous usage data: May be retained in aggregated, non-identifiable form indefinitely.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Row-level security (RLS) policies on our database to ensure users can only access their own data
  • Secure credential storage using industry-standard authentication providers
  • No storage of raw passwords — authentication handled by Supabase Auth

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
